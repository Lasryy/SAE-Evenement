"Concours d'élégance automobile 2024"

The town of Enghien-les-Bains is widely recognized for its sophisticated and refined atmosphere, and this reputation extends to its "Concours d'Élégance". In 2024, this prestigious event is generating considerable interest as it showcases a diverse range of vintage and contemporary vehicles, each competing for the esteemed distinction of being crowned the embodiment of style.

At the 2024 Automobile Salon, a diverse selection of remarkable vehicles awaits participants, featuring iconic car models from the 1940s as well as post-war cars. Vehicles from renowned brands like Jaguar, Porsche, and Citroën are proudly displayed by their owners, who have meticulously restored and perfectly preserved them.

Enghien-les-Bains, with its charming tree-lined streets and sophisticated ambiance, provided the ideal spot for this occasion. The vehicles were exhibited by the tranquil lakeside, creating a breathtaking scene for this esteemed gathering.

The range of cars presented at the event delighted participants and spectators alike, from legendary sports cars like the iconic Ford Mustang to antique automobiles such as the Ford T. Each vehicle stands as a masterpiece, showcasing the craftsmanship and dedication of those who have maintained them.

The challenging task of choosing the winner in a highly competitive field falls to a jury composed of automotive specialists and individuals from the design field. Each automobile is subjected to meticulous evaluation based on rigorous criteria encompassing design, originality, and preservation of historical significance.

The winners were honored and congratulated for their role in preserving automotive heritage during the awards ceremony. However, the true victory extends far beyond the applause, as it lies within the fervent community that comes together to celebrate their collective adoration for cars and their timeless elegance.



The 2024 Concours d'Élégance offers a rich and varied program centered around vintage vehicles. It took place on May 18 and 19, 2024, with distinct activities for each day.

Saturday, May 18, 2024:

• Exhibition of vintage vehicles (2 PM to 6 PM) : This exhibition, with free access, was held at the lake pier and the Thermes forecourt.

• Discovery rides aboard vintage vehicles (3 PM to 5 PM) : Visitors could embark on a discovery ride starting from the Thermes forecourt. Tickets were €5 for one person, €8 for two, and €10 for three, with proceeds donated to the charitable works of the Lions Club of Enghien-Montmorency.

Sunday, May 19, 2024:

• Parade of vintage vehicles (11 AM to 12 PM) : A parade through the city center and around the lake allowed spectators to admire the vehicles in motion and hear the sound of their engines.

• "Concours d'Élégance Automobile" (3 PM to 5 PM) : The competition began on Sunday at 3 PM. All vehicles paraded and were presented both aesthetically and historically. The competition concluded with an awards ceremony at 5 PM at the lake pier. Access was free for all.

Entertainment throughout the weekend:

• Saturday and Sunday (2 PM to 6 PM) at the lake pier: Jazz bands, giant wooden games, the presence of a portrait artist and a caricaturist, as well as painting workshops, offered varied entertainment for all families.

Photo exhibition from Monday, April 29:

• Retrospective of the Concours d'Élégance Automobile and vintage automobiles: All vehicles presented and participating in the competition could be admired from April 29 near the event location. This photographic exhibition was held at the Kiosk of the Rose Garden, with free access.
