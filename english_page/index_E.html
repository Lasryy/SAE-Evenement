The programme for 2025

Return of the Enghien-les-Bains Automobile Elegance Contest, taking place on May 17 and 18, 2025. You will be able to admire numerous pre- and post-war vintage cars competing for the famous Elegance Prize, awarded on Sunday by a jury of amateurs and professionals according to specific criteria (car model, crew, overall presentation…).

Saturday Program
From 2 PM to 6 PM :
Exhibition of vintage cars on the Lake Pier and the Thermal Baths Square
From 3 PM to 5 PM :
Discovery rides aboard vintage vehicles

Departure from the Thermal Baths Square – Rates: €5 (1 person), €8 (2 people), €10 (3 people).
The proceeds will benefit the charitable works of the Lions Club of Enghien-Montmorency.

Sunday Program
From 11 AM to 12 PM :
Parade of vintage cars through the town
From 3 PM to 5 PM :
Automobile Elegance Contest in front of the Lake Pier
Prize-giving ceremony at 5 PM.

Throughout the weekend, a retrospective exhibition of Enghien-les-Bains automobile elegance contests will be on display at the Kiosk of the Rose Garden.

As you can see, this "Elegance in Automobiles" event, organized by the Tourist Office, continues the tradition of automobile elegance contests held in Enghien since 1948. A visit to the spa town is highly recommended.

Saturday, May 18, 2024, from 2 PM to 6 PM and Sunday, May 19, 2024, from 11 AM to 5 PM - Lake Pier and Thermal Baths Square, 69 General de Gaulle Street, Enghien – Free entrance.
